Hi, I am Lilia Pianella

Frontend Developer, based in Denmark.

I am a front-end developer based in Denmark, using code as a creative medium to build practical projects that respond to real needs. I enjoy building responsive, accessible interfaces using Bootstrap and Responsive Design Techniques for modern websites, and I do so daily with tools like VS Code and GitHub.

With a background in Sustainable Development, Global Health, and Natural Sciences - especially Food Science - my work is rooted in empathy and care, for both human and more-than-human worlds. I thrive in collaborative environments, approach challenges with creativity and optimism, and believe technology and meaningful human connection together can contribute to a more caring and sustainable world.
